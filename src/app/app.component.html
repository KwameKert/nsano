About Instant Credit

Instant Credit is a service that enables real-time termination of remittances to mobile wallets and bank accounts in Africa in fast, secure and convenient manner.

Instant Credit offers APIs directly integrated into the mobile money platforms, of MNOs and all banks via standard security protocols. This allows for direct querying and veriﬁcation of customers’ mobile money accounts (active status) and bank accounts for the speciﬁed transactions.

  1. Instant Credit offers APIs directly integrated into the mobile money platforms of MNOs and all banks via standard security protocols. This allows for direct querying and verification of customers’ mobile money accounts (active status) and bank accounts for the specified transactions.

  2. A virtual account is created for the IMT on the Instant Credit.

  3. Funds are transferred by the IMT to the Instant Credit bank account and the IMT virtual account is immediately credited with the commensurate amount in local currency to allow for real-time termination to either wallets or bank accounts.

Benefits of working with Instant credit

APIs Embedded in a Fraud Detection & Prevention Service

All transactions go through our fraud platform powered by Artificial intelligence and Machine learning and designed to detect and prevent the processing of fraudulent transactions. The platform can be customized to create fraud rules based on patterns and trends required.

Simple Integration

A single integration to Instant Credit allows you access to existing mobile money platforms and banks accounts as well as potential ones. Thus, relieving TransferWise from the hassle of establishing and managing connectivity to all MNOs and banks across the continent.


Real-time Processing and Seamless Integration

With Instant Credit, you are assured of real-time processing of all transactions and a dedicated integrations team to ensure a smooth and easy integration process.

Reconciliation

Daily reconciliations are done for all bank and wallet transactions with daily reports sent for all transactions.



Transparency

There is total transparency on the all transactions through the tracking portal. The tracking portal allows you to;
- Track real time updates of transactions
- Generate reports for reconciliation and settlements.


Security

Impersonation is near to impossible using our system since the customer is identified by a unique phone number (MSISDN) and or account number which can be verified with both the bank and telecom operator. Upon verification, funds are sent directly into the customers wallet/account with no third party interference.
